## Getting Started

### What is ProjectGuide?

ProjectGuide is a **methodology-driven project management platform** that helps you manage projects using proven PM frameworks:
- üìä **Traditional (Waterfall)** - Sequential approach with 94 comprehensive tasks
- üîÑ **Hybrid** - Blended approach combining traditional and agile
- ‚ö° **Agile (Scrum)** - Sprint-based iterative approach

**Key Differentiator:** Unlike generic PM tools, ProjectGuide provides built-in guidance for each task, teaching you PM best practices while you work.

### Quick Start (5 Minutes)

1. **Launch the App**
   - Open ProjectGuide in your web browser
   - Bookmark it for easy access (it works offline after first load!)

2. **Choose Your Methodology**
   - Click "Get Started" on the landing page
   - Select Traditional, Hybrid, or Agile based on your project needs
   - Not sure? Start with **Traditional** for comprehensive coverage

3. **Explore the Interface**
   - Left sidebar: Navigate between project phases
   - Main area: View and manage tasks
   - Top bar: Access quick actions (Dashboard, Team, Templates, Export)

4. **Create Your First Task**
   - Click on any task to view details
   - Toggle task status: To Do ‚Üí In Progress ‚Üí Completed
   - Add notes, set due dates, assign team members

5. **Track Your Progress**
   - Click the Dashboard button (top-right)
   - View progress by phase and knowledge area
   - See team workload distribution

### Your First Project (15 Minutes)

**Scenario:** Let's set up a website redesign project

1. **Select Traditional Methodology** (most comprehensive for learning)

2. **Navigate to "Initiating" Phase**
   - Find it in the left sidebar
   - This is where every project begins

3. **Complete the Project Charter**
   - Click on "Develop Project Charter" task
   - Read the guidance (toggle "Show Guidance")
   - Mark as "In Progress"
   - Click "Templates" ‚Üí "Project Charter" to download template
   - Fill out the template (you can do this outside the app)
   - Add notes about your project charter in the task

4. **Identify Stakeholders**
   - Find "Identify Stakeholders" task
   - Click "Team" in the top navigation
   - Add your actual team members
   - Assign this task to yourself or your Business Analyst

5. **Set a Due Date**
   - Click the calendar icon on any task
   - Choose a due date
   - View all due dates in Calendar View (toggle at top)

6. **View Your Progress**
   - Click "Dashboard" (top-right bar chart icon)
   - See your first tasks marked complete!
   - Track percentage progress

## Core Features

### 1. Methodology Selection

**Traditional (Waterfall)**
- **Best for:** Well-defined projects, regulatory environments, fixed requirements
- **7 Phases:** Orientation ‚Üí Planning ‚Üí Executing ‚Üí Monitoring ‚Üí Closing
- **94 Tasks** covering all aspects of PM
- **Example projects:** Construction, government contracts, compliance projects

**Hybrid**
- **Best for:** Projects needing structure but with some uncertainty
- **Combines:** Traditional planning with agile execution
- **Iterative sprints** within structured phases
- **Example projects:** Product launches, marketing campaigns, process improvements

**Agile (Scrum)**
- **Best for:** Software development, innovative products, changing requirements
- **Sprint-based:** 2-4 week iterations
- **Continuous feedback** and adaptation
- **Example projects:** Software apps, startups, R&D projects

### 2. Task Management

**Task Statuses:**
- üîµ **To Do** - Not started yet
- üü° **In Progress** - Currently working on it
- üü¢ **Completed** - Done!

**Task Actions:**
- **Click task name** - Toggle status (To Do ‚Üí In Progress ‚Üí Completed)
- **Add Notes** - Click note icon to document details
- **Set Due Date** - Click calendar icon to add deadline
- **Assign Team Member** - Select from your team
- **View Guidance** - Toggle "Show Guidance" for PM best practices

### 3. Knowledge Areas (PMBOK Framework)

ProjectGuide organizes tasks by 10 PM Knowledge Areas:

1. üîµ **Integration** - Coordinating all aspects of the project
2. üü£ **Scope** - Defining what's included/excluded
3. üîµ **Schedule** - Planning and controlling timelines
4. üü† **Cost** - Budgeting and cost control
5. üü¢ **Quality** - Ensuring deliverables meet standards
6. üî¥ **Resource** - Managing team and materials
7. üü£ **Communications** - Stakeholder engagement
8. üî¥ **Risk** - Identifying and mitigating threats
9. üü¢ **Procurement** - Vendor and contract management
10. üîµ **Stakeholder** - Managing expectations and engagement

**Filter by Knowledge Area:**
- Use the "Knowledge Area" dropdown to focus on specific areas
- Great for studying for PMP/CAPM certification!

### 4. Team Management

**Add Team Members:**
1. Click "Team" in top navigation
2. Click "Add Team Member"
3. Enter name, role, and email
4. Click "Add"

**Assign Tasks:**
- Click the person icon on any task
- Select team member from dropdown
- View workload distribution in Team dashboard

**Team Dashboard:**
- See who's assigned to what
- Balance workload across team
- Identify overallocation

### 5. Calendar & Due Dates

**Calendar View:**
- Toggle "Calendar" at the top of the dashboard
- View tasks by date
- See today highlighted
- Navigate months with arrows

**Setting Due Dates:**
- Click calendar icon on task
- Choose date
- Tasks appear in Calendar View
- Use Quick Filters ‚Üí "Due This Week" to see upcoming deadlines

### 6. Progress Dashboard

**Access:**
- Click bar chart icon (top right)
- Or click "Dashboard" button

**Metrics Shown:**
- **Overall Progress** - Percentage and task count
- **Progress by Phase** - Visual bars for each project phase
- **Progress by Knowledge Area** - Track coverage across PM domains
- **Team Workload** - See task distribution

**Use Cases:**
- Status reports for stakeholders
- Identify bottlenecks
- Balance team workload
- Study progress for certification prep

### 7. Document Templates

**6 Professional Templates:**
1. **Project Charter** - Formal project authorization
2. **Business Case** - Justification and ROI analysis
3. **Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)** - Hierarchical task decomposition
4. **Risk Register** - Risk identification and mitigation plans
5. **Stakeholder Register** - Stakeholder analysis and engagement
6. **Communications Plan** - How and when to communicate

**How to Use:**
1. Click "Templates" (top navigation)
2. Click "Download" on any template
3. Edit in your preferred app (supports Markdown)
4. Save completed templates alongside your project
5. Reference them as you complete related tasks

### 8. Export & Reporting

**Export Options:**
1. **Summary Report** - High-level progress overview
2. **Detailed Report** - Complete task list with metadata
3. **Outstanding Tasks Report** - Grouped by assignee
4. **Full Data Export** - JSON backup of entire project

**How to Export:**
1. Click "Export" button (top right)
2. Choose report type
3. File downloads immediately
4. Reports are in Markdown format (human-readable, version-control friendly)

### 9. Data Persistence & Backup

**Automatic Saving:**
- All changes save instantly to your browser's local storage
- No internet required after initial load
- Data persists across browser sessions

**Important:** Your data is stored locally on your device. To access from another device, use the Export/Import feature:

1. **Export:** Click "Export" ‚Üí "Full Data Export"
2. **Import:** Click "Import Data" ‚Üí Select your JSON file

**Backup Recommendations:**
- Export your data weekly
- Store backups in cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox)
- Import data when switching devices

### 10. Dark Mode

- Click the sun/moon icon (top right)
- Reduces eye strain for extended use
- Preference saves automatically

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes! Your data is stored locally on your device. We don't have access to it. Make sure to export backups regularly.

**Q: Can I use ProjectGuide offline?**
A: Yes! After the first load, the app works completely offline. Your data is stored locally.

**Q: Can multiple people work on the same project?**
A: Currently, ProjectGuide is single-user. Each person has their own project data. We're working on real-time collaboration for a future release.

**Q: How do I share my project with my team?**
A: Use Export ‚Üí "Full Data Export" to download your project. Share the JSON file with team members, who can import it. (Note: This creates a copy, not a live shared project.)

**Q: Is ProjectGuide free?**
A: Yes! The beta is completely free. We're exploring paid plans for the future with additional features (cloud sync, collaboration, file attachments).

### Features

**Q: Can I upload files or attachments?**
A: Not yet. File attachments are on our roadmap. For now, use task notes to link to files in cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.).

**Q: Can I customize tasks or add my own?**
A: Not in this version. You can add notes to existing tasks to customize them for your needs. Custom tasks are planned for future releases.

**Q: Can I have multiple projects?**
A: Currently, you can have one active project per methodology. To switch projects, export your current project and import a different one.

**Q: Does ProjectGuide integrate with other tools?**
A: Not yet. Integrations (Slack, Jira, Google Calendar, etc.) are on our roadmap.

**Q: Can I print or export to PDF?**
A: Reports export as Markdown (.md files). You can convert Markdown to PDF using online tools or apps like Typora, Obsidian, or VS Code.

### Technical

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: All modern browsers:
- Chrome/Edge (recommended)
- Firefox
- Safari
- Opera

**Q: How much data can I store?**
A: Browser local storage typically allows 5-10MB, which is more than enough for project data. You're unlikely to hit this limit.

**Q: What if I clear my browser cache?**
A: Your ProjectGuide data will be deleted. Always keep recent exports as backups!

**Q: Does ProjectGuide work on mobile?**
A: Yes! The interface is responsive and works on tablets and phones. For best experience, use a tablet or desktop.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Not yet. A native mobile app is on our roadmap.

### Certification & Learning

**Q: Will ProjectGuide help me pass the PMP exam?**
A: ProjectGuide teaches practical application of PMBOK concepts, which complements exam prep courses. It's excellent for hands-on practice, but pair it with a dedicated PMP study course for exam-specific content.

**Q: Is ProjectGuide aligned with the PMBOK Guide?**
A: Yes! ProjectGuide is built on PMBOK 6th Edition (10 Knowledge Areas, 5 Process Groups). We're adding 7th Edition support in a future release.

**Q: Can I earn PDUs using ProjectGuide?**
A: ProjectGuide itself doesn't award PDUs, but using it for real project work counts toward PDU credits under "Working as a Practitioner" category (maximum 8 PDUs per year).
